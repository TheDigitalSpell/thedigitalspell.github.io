## Master the Setup

The first step to solving any mystery is understanding the clues. Equadeck helps you visualize the problem before you even start calculating.

Each level presents clear variable descriptions at the top of the screen. Your job is to connect those descriptions with the correct values (p, q, and r) in the solving area below. It's the perfect way to understand *what* the equation means before you try to solve it.

---

## Solve Like a Detective

Once the equation is set up, the real action begins!

Equadeck guides you through the logical process of isolating x using **inverse operations**. Need to undo an addition? Subtract! Is there a multiplication? Divide!

You'll learn to make strategic decisions step by step, building your math confidence with every correct move.

## Instant Feedback to Grow

Made a mistake? No problem! True learning happens when we correct our errors.

Equadeck features an instant visual feedback system to keep you on track. If you get it right, you'll see a **yellow checkmark** and earn a star for your score. If you slip up, the game shows a **red cross** (error) alongside a blue circle pointing out the correction.

Learn from the mistake, keep an eye on your life points, and try again until you master the equation!
