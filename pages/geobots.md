**Welcome to GeoBots, a collection of interactive adventures designed for curious minds aged 3-6.**

The GeoBots are friendly robots on a mission to explore our planet. Through their eyes, children discover new cultures, solve physics-based puzzles, and learn about the world in a safe, stress-free environment.
